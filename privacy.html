NexionEditor — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 10, 2026 · Operated by Nexion Digital (nexion.digital)

1. What the editor processes locally

Video, audio and image files you import are processed entirely in your browser. Projects and templates are stored locally in your browser's storage (IndexedDB). We run no analytics and set no tracking cookies.

2. Publishing to TikTok and Instagram

If you choose to connect a social account, the platform's official OAuth flow is used. We then store, on our server infrastructure (Cloudflare):

  • the OAuth access token and the connected account's basic profile info (username / display name) — used solely to publish on your request and to show which account is connected;
  • the exported video file you chose to publish — uploaded transiently so the platform can fetch it, then removable at any time.

Uploads happen only when you explicitly click "Post". TikTok videos are delivered as drafts to your TikTok inbox and become public only after you confirm inside the TikTok app. We never read your messages, followers, or other account data, and we never post without your action.

3. Sharing and selling

We do not sell, rent, or share your data or content with third parties, other than transmitting the video and caption to the platform you asked us to publish to (TikTok / Meta), under their terms.

4. Retention and deletion

Access tokens remain stored until you disconnect the account or ask us to remove them. Uploaded videos are kept only as long as needed for publishing. To request deletion of any stored data, email us and we will remove it promptly.
